Short Bio

Hello! I am a final-year undergraduate student at the University of Hong Kong (HKU). I am working on large language models and interpretable machine learning. Previously, I was fortunate to work with Byron Wallace during my exchange at Northeastern University (NEU) and collaborated with Zhonghua Liu and Lequan Yu at HKU. I also spend a wonderful time at Microsoft Research Asia working with Shuming Ma.
